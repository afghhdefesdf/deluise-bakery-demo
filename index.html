WHO WE ARE

Since 1938.

Before the interstate, before the mall, before your grandparents met — there was a bakery on Chalkstone Avenue. The DeLuise family opened its doors in Providence in 1938, and has been part of the neighborhood's birthdays, graduations, weddings, holidays, and ordinary Tuesdays ever since.

In 2012, the family fired up a second oven at 701 Oaklawn Avenue in Cranston. Same recipes. Same hands-in-the-dough way of doing things. Twice the neighborhoods.

We are proud to be your neighbors — and proud to still be baking, 88 years on.
